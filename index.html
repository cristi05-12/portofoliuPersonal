Who I Am

The person behind the terminal.

I'm Cristian Vanica, an IT student with a deep passion for how networks work under the hood. My journey started with a simple curiosity about how computers talk to each other — and quickly evolved into building full home lab environments.

I spend most of my free time in virtual machines: configuring Active Directory domains, routing packets across simulated Cisco topologies, and hardening Linux servers. I believe the best way to learn infrastructure is to break it, fix it, and document everything.
